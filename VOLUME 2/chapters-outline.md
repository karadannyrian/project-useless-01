| Chapter | POV | Setting | Key Beats |

## Part Five: The Flight — Blood and Sand

*Running from the Order. Running from the Dominion. Running from the girl's own power. The border is three days away. It might as well be a lifetime.*

| Chapter | POV | Setting | Key Beats |
|---------|-----|---------|-----------|
| 26 | Alex | Desert trail | The group travels. Seres is unconscious, wrapped in blankets. Alex watches her — the heat-scars, the tremor in her hand, the wrongness of her debt. She's terrified and fascinated. |
| 27 | Hark | Desert trail | Seres wakes. Hark is carrying her. She doesn't know where they're going. She asks: *"Are you going to hurt me?"* Hark says: *"No."* She asks: *"Why should I believe you?"* Hark says nothing. He doesn't have an answer. |
| 28 | Alex | Gilded Eye (inland sea) | The Order catches up. A Savant and her Silencers. Alex sees them from a distance — reads their debt. The Savant is clean (Insensitive). The Silencers carry the grey of old kills. She warns the group. They hide. The Savant passes within fifty feet. |
| 29 | Hark | Gilded Eye, aftermath | The Order passes. Hark's hand is on his knife the whole time. He doesn't draw. He doesn't breathe. After, Renne finds his hand. Holds it. He doesn't pull away. |
| 30 | Alex | Ash-wraith canyon | Monster attack. Ash-wraiths — remnants of old mages, debt given form. Hark's fire feeds them. Seres, barely conscious, reaches out. She burns one. The debt annihilates. The wraith dissolves. Hark sees. Alex sees. No one speaks. |

## Epilogue

**POV:** Hark (closing the volume as it began — his voice, his lens)

**Setting:** A ship, leaving the Dominion. The Archipelago is somewhere ahead. The farm is behind them. Everything is behind them.

**Key Beats:**
- Hark stands at the rail. The water is grey. His hands are cold.
- Renne joins him. Her hand on his. She doesn't speak.
- Seres is below deck, asleep. The heat-scars on her ribs are healing. The tremor in her hand will never leave.
- Troy and Alex are at the other end of the ship. They're talking. Quietly. Hark doesn't listen. He doesn't need to.
- Hark thinks about the farm. The ropewalk room. The carriage. The silk. Lea. Joel. The network. The girl who burned herself on his debt.
- He doesn't have a word for what he's feeling. He doesn't need one.
- Renne says: *"We'll find somewhere."*
- Hark says: *"We always do."*
- The ship sails on.
