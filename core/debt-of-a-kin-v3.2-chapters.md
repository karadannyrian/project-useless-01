### PROLOGUE: THE FOUNDLING HOUSE

**POV:** Renne (third‑person, unnamed until later)
**Setting:** The Cradle – Order foundling house. Rain. Midnight.
**Length:** ~1,500 words

**Scene breakdown:**

1. A young woman (Renne, 17) stands outside a grey stone building. She holds a newborn wrapped in clean cloth – Alexandria.
2. She walks inside. A silent Sister meets her.
   - Sister: "Name?"
   - Renne: "Alexandria."
   - Sister: "Your name?"
   - Renne: "No one."
3. Renne hands over the baby. Her hands – already scarred from healing debt – tremble.
4. She watches the Sister carry Alexandria to a cot. The baby doesn't cry.
5. Renne walks out into the rain. She does not look back.
6. On a muddy road, she presses her hands to her chest and whispers: *"I'll find you again. I swear it."*

**Debt note:** The childbirth debt is recorded in Renne's file, buried deep. Team 3 will find it fifteen years later.

### PART ONE: THE BOARD IS SET

#### CHAPTER 1: THE CONTRACT

**POV:** Hark
**Setting:** Crown of Winter – a tavern. Midnight. Snow outside.
**Length:** ~2,500 words

**Opening line:** *"The contract said three. I'd found four."*

**Scene breakdown:**

1. Hark sits in a corner. He runs his observational hierarchy: hands of every patron, exits, weapons.
2. A hooded figure (Kellis, a broker) approaches. Hark has a Red Mark bounty on his head.
3. Kellis offers a job: find a woman known as "the Seed." Last seen in the Grain‑Lords' Confederacy. Pays in debt‑null fragments.
4. Internal monologue (FID rules):
   - *"He considered the possibility that Kellis was lying."* (Trust)
   - *"His pulse quickened. His hand found the knife."* (Fear)
   - *"The debt‑null fragments gleamed. His chest ached. He looked away."* (Want)
5. Hark takes the job. Doesn't know why.
6. He leaves the tavern. Snow fills his tracks.

**Closing line:** *"He didn't ask himself why he was going. The rules forbade the question."*

#### CHAPTER 2: THE SCALPEL

**POV:** Alexandria
**Setting:** The Cradle – training grounds. Morning.
**Length:** ~2,200 words

**Opening line:** *"The dummy had no hands. That was the problem."*

**Scene breakdown:**

1. Alexandria (15) practices knife work on a training dummy. Her instructor, Master Vellen, critiques her form.
2. She's good – unnaturally good – but she hesitates before killing strikes. Vellen calls it "mercy." Alex calls it "geometry."
3. Enter Orin: an old Savant with a ledger under his arm. He's assigned as her partner.
   - Orin: *"You're the scalpel. I'm the map. Don't cut until I tell you."*
4. Alex processes him as threat geometry: exits, hands, weight shift.
5. She thinks about the foundling house. The cold beds. The silent meals.
6. Orin hands her a file: "The Seed." Debt Level: Amber. Mission: capture or terminate.

**Closing line:** *"She didn't know what 'lonely' meant. The Cradle had never taught her."*

#### CHAPTER 3: THE GHOST

**POV:** Hark
**Setting:** Crown of Winter – a frozen road. Dawn.
**Length:** ~2,000 words

**Scene breakdown:**

1. Hark travels toward the Grain‑Lords' Confederacy. He joins a trader caravan for a day.
2. He listens to rumours: a healer in the south who doesn't use Order‑sanctioned methods. A woman who takes in Sensitive children. The Order has sent five teams.
   - Trader: *"They call her the Seed. Plants something in the kids. Hope, I heard."*
   - Hark: (silence)
3. A memory fragment surfaces: a young woman's hands – small, scarred, gentle – pressing a cloth to a wound on his arm. Her voice: *"Hold still. This will hurt."*
4. Hark doesn't know where the memory came from. His chest tightens. He counts the miles to the horizon.
5. He decides to find the Seed.

**Closing line:** *"He remembered her hands before he remembered her face. That was the problem with memories – they remembered what he'd forbidden himself to name."*

#### CHAPTER 4: THE HEALER

**POV:** Renne
**Setting:** Grain‑Lords' Confederacy – a farmhouse repurposed as a clinic.
**Length:** ~3,000 words

**Opening line:** *"The morning clinic was for broken bones and lies."*

**Scene breakdown – Morning:**

1. Renne tends to local farmers – mundane medicine only. No magic.
   - A man with a dislocated shoulder.
   - A child with a fever.
   - An old woman with cataracts.
2. Her hands tremble when she reaches for surgical thread. Healing debt. Always there.

**Scene breakdown – Afternoon:**

1. Two Sensitive children (boy 10, girl 8) arrive for a secret lesson.
2. Renne teaches them to feel their internal reservoirs without drawing from them.
   - *"Magic is borrowing. Every spell leaves a scar. The only safe magic is the magic you don't cast."*

**Scene breakdown – Evening:**

1. Marta the courier arrives with news: a new Order team in the area – Team 5, the Quiet Death.
2. Renne sends the children to the root cellar.
3. Internal monologue: She thinks of Alexandria. Would be fifteen now. Old enough to hold a knife. *She pushed the thought away.* (She cannot afford to want.)

**Closing line:** *"She lit a candle. She waited for the dark to bring its hunters."*

#### CHAPTER 5: THE SEED'S FIRST ALLY

**POV:** Renne
**Setting:** Marta's tavern. Evening.
**Length:** ~2,000 words

**Scene breakdown:**
